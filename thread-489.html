get_iplayer doesn't search for individual episodes. Your search criteria are checked against every entry in the cache and all matches are returned. If you're trying to match a single episode from a single series, you need very specific criteria, but it still may not be possible. You shouldn't be attempting such a specific search in the PVR anyway. It will only ever match once, and the PVR is really for repeated searching to pick up entire series.
