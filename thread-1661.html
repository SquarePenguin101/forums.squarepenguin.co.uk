After installing "get_iplayer-3.18.0-setup.exe" in Windows XP SP3, you have to put manually ffmpeg.exe into the "c:\Program Files\get_iplayer\utils" folder

if your vesion of ffmpeg gives you this error in running: "procedure entry point getnumanodeprocessormaskex could not be located in the dynamic link library kernel32.dll" as shown here[Image: https://i.ytimg.com/vi/K5uM0RFcriM/hqdefault.jpg]

leaving ts video files which are not converted to mp4 format automatically, then put this ffmpeg.exe into the \utils folder instead from ffmpeg-2.5.2-win32-static.7z (This is ffmpeg from Dec2014)
