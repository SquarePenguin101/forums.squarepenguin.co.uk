You need to update to the latest version of 2.90. This includes a lot of fixes for problems that have developed since the original 2.90 was released.

Have a look at https://squarepenguin.co.uk/wiki/install...itheadgit/ and if you are on Windows https://squarepenguin.co.uk/wiki/install...itheadwin/

Once you have updated, the programme should download.
