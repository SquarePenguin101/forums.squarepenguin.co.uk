It turns out that your case isn't related to that bug. Because that series is currently going out on BBC One (didn't notice that before), get_iplayer sees it in the schedule info used to populate the cache, which says that Episode 5 has not yet been broadcast. Even though the iPlayer already makes the episode available, get_iplayer's cache search results override that. Box sets that aren't going out on a linear service don't have that ambiguity. Bottom line: you would always need to use --future to get already-available episodes in advance of broadcast date.
