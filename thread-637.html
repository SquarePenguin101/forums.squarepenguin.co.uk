Not a get_iplayer issue, but rather a problem on your system.  From your post:

get_iplayer Output:

Failed to open file! E:\Media\iplayer input\Silent_Witness_Series_19_-_3a._Life_
Licence_Part_1_b06ykmc0_default.partial.mp4.flv
That should be a big flashing warning sign that something is wrong with your output location: disk full, not mounted, wrong permissions, etc. get_iplayer can't know what the problem is, so you'll have to investigate.

user-1170

22-01-2016, 06:33 PM
Fixed it!
Just as you said it was a permissions issue, probably got messed up during the upgrade.
Fixed it by giving the 'Everyone' group write permission for my output folder.
Thanks
