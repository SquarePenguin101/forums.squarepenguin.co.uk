Relax. You said you have 3 days.

Anyway, the error message you included suggests you do not have RTMPdump installed. This might point to a failed installation of get_iplayer and it's dependencies.

I would recommend that you uninstall your current version of get_iplayer and reinstall it and ALL the various dependencies (additional programmes) that are required. You can find the installer on the download page here:

https://squarepenguin.co.uk/guides/windo...all-guide/

The installer will automatically install all the required additional programmes. Do NOT untick any of those additional programmes during the install process, even if you have something like VLC installed already.

If this error continues after a clean reinstall then there could be something interfering with RTMPdump and we can begin to investigate that.
