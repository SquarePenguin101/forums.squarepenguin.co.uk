It is not an area restriction. The various installer components are retrieved from different locations not in the UK, and since they all fail to download for you, that suggests the culprit must be on your machine. A first guess would be that you have a third-party firewall, anti-virus, content filter, security application or something similar that is interfering with network access for the installer. Ring any bells?
