Resuming works fine so long as the same streams can be accessed. Your system obviously can't manage it. Judging from your description, your connection is simply too poor. Download at lower quality and/or in smaller time segments. There will be a few seconds of overlap between segments, so you would need to do some minor editing if you want a single file.
