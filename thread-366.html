It's never worked outside the UK (EDIT: For TV). BBC iPlayer is a geo-restricted service that requires a UK IP address to access. This is nothing to do with get_iplayer but a function of the BBC iPlayer service itself.

The message you are seeing is simply due to a change to the error message in the recent release informing you that the error may be because you are outside the UK. Previously there was no error message but the download failed still. So, the reason you didn't see it before is because it didn't exist, not because get_iplayer is restricting you.

EDIT: I see this was about Radio, my bad for making the assumption it was TV. Radio should work after updating to the latest version of get_iplayer.
