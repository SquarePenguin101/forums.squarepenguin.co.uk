The podcast index data is indeed gone.  No great surprise - that data source was obsolete long ago, so get_iplayer has been living on borrowed time. I planned to deprecate and then remove podcast support from get_iplayer anyway, but it appears the BBC may have beaten me to it.  If the podcast index doesn't reappear within a week, it is probably gone for good.
