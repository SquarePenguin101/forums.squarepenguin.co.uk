The file names l0ok OK, which is as far as get_iplayer can take you. Now you need to set up a folder hierarchy and add the show-level metadata (get_iplayer only produces metadata for individual episodes). Go to the Kodi wiki and look at the page on naming video files/TV shows and also look at the pages with examples of NFO files for tv shows and episodes.
