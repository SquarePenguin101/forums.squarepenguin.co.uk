Updated information re: 320K TV downloads

user-1980

17-07-2019, 04:25 PM
The new format of suffix in playlist URLs with 320K audio is:

-audio_eng=320000-video=xxxxxxx.m3u8

Previously, I believe it was:

-audio=320000-video=xxxxxxx.m3u8

Thanks.
