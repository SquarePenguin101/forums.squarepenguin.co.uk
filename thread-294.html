As you are here in the OS X forums I'm going to assume you're running OS X. There isn't an installer per se for OS X, though there are options with HOmebrew and MacPorts.

The best guide to get things installed on OS X is probably here:

https://squarepenguin.co.uk/guides/mac-o...all-guide/

You have to just follow it step by step. You don't have to put much thought into why you are downloading the things it tells you to, or why you're using the commands you are being told to (unless you want to that is) and it should get you updated to the latest version. You're effectively installing the latest version over the current one, thereby upgrading it.
