You just need to be a bit more patient. You posted less than an hour after the film finished, and sometimes it takes longer than that for programmes to appear in the data feeds used by get_iplayer, even if the programme is already available on your TV. Searching for the PID won't help because it won't be in the programme cache, either. If you know the PID but the programme is not yet available in the cache, you should try it directly from the command line with
Code:
--pid
.
