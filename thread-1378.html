Nothing to do with punctuation. The programme hasn't yet been broadcast, so you're not going to find it in a search unless you use --future (if you've added future listings to your cache) EDIT: or - ironically - if it is not in your cache at all. That is a weakness of get_iplayer. It is a bit of a problem with BBC Three programmes that are being broadcast on linear services.
