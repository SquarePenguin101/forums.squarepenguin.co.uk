21-04-2015, 02:01 PM
No, the BBC isn't doing anything. Those programmes work fine, just not for you. If the audio described version is the only version in search results for Poldark, then delete
Code:
%USERPROFILE%\.get_iplayer\tv.cache
and rebuild it with
Code:
get_iplayer --refresh
. If that doesn't work, post the output from
Code:
get_iplayer Poldark
and
Code:
get_iplayer --show-options Poldark
, scrubbing your username if necessary.
