21-02-2016, 04:37 PM
You definitely don't need it, especially if you don't know what it is. And lest you be tempted to experiment, be advised that the localfiles programme type is scheduled for removal. The error message was generated because you used --type=all, which includes the localfiles programme type. In general, avoid --type=all. It too is scheduled for removal. For now, it's enough to do what you have done and remove the the localfiles.plugin file. It will be removed in the next release, anyway.
