"p02g70hx" is a group PID and it doesn't seem to have metadata with it.

If you just want "Jungle Green : Borneo" use "p00hhrf6" which is the programmes PID. You will also need to add --playlist-metadata to your command for it to download.

No.
Code:
--playlist-metadata
is a workaround for a bug that affects a few programmes. You can't download from the PID for a group, like the one you posted.
