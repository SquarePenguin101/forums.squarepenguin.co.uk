I use a straightforward command such as get_iplayer --type=radio > current_date.txt to give me a list of currently available programmes on iplayer (in this example onÂ radio). However, this and similar commands only seem to give me programmes for the last 7 days, whereas they are now available for the last 30 days. user-585

15-10-2015, 12:07 AM
Anything over 7 days old needs to be downloaded by PID. The feeds get_iplayer uses to build the cache only hold info about programmes from the last 7 days. The option to build a 30 day cache over the course of a few weeks may be available soon https://github.com/get-iplayer/get_iplayer/issues/195
