Ignore the "Invalid HLS playlist" errors. They usually occur when an HLS playlist fails to download, which means you either have connection problems upstream or the BBC servers have problems. It doesn't matter since you are not using HLS to download. Your immediate problem is that you neglected to remove a previous partial download. If you don't do that, you'll never get past the corrupt file warning.
