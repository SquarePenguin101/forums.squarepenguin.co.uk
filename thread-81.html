Audio Description recording problems

user-276

09-12-2013, 02:49 PM
I'm having problems recording Audio Descriptive programmes.

I enter get_iplayer "http://www.bbc.co.uk/iplayer/episode/b03lbnwx/ad/Atlantis_The_Price_of_Hope/" and while get_iplayer recognizes and records the link 100% there is no descriptive track. I have to use RTMPDumpHelper which works fine for anything below an hour, anything over and it doesn't work, well not for me.

Any help?

user-2

09-12-2013, 03:00 PM
You need to add
Code:
--versions=audiodescribed
to your command. See:

https://squarepenguin.co.uk/wiki/options...ch-options

get_iplayer doesn't search for audio described or signed programme versions (where available) unless explicitly instructed to do so.
