user-585

12-08-2016, 02:13 PM
Are you referring to the message that looks something like this?

get_iplayer Output:

INFO: Steve Wright in the Afternoon (b07jck37) Already in history (C:\Users\Computer/get_iplayer/download_history) - use --force to override
If that is what you mean, they are not being downloaded to that folder. That's where get_iplayer's history file is stored which keeps track of what has been downloaded in the past. What that's telling you is that the radio programme that you are trying to download has been downloaded to your computer in the past. If you want to download it again use --force as suggested.
