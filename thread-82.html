I wonder if anyone can help with me out. I have successfully downloaded all of the smiley radio series except one episode. "tinker, tailor, soldier, spy" part 2. Whenever I try to Get-iplayer advises me that I have already downloaded this particular episode. The problem was that the first time I tried to download this episode my computer crashed (not related to Get-iplayer) and I only had a partial download which would not work. When I looked on the forums it reccommnended deleting failed download and starting again. 09-12-2013, 10:45 PM
The programme is still in your download history, so you need to delete the partial download and add
Code:
--force
to your command line to override the entry in download history. See:

https://squarepenguin.co.uk/wiki/options...ng-options
