You don't say how you installed get_iplayer 2.91. I can only surmise that you attempted a Homebrew installation on top of a manually installed 2.91. I suggest you completely uninstall Homebrew and any applications installed with it (assuming you only installed Homebrew for get_iplayer). See http://docs.brew.sh/FAQ.html for information on completely uninstalling Homebrew. With that done, perform a fresh manual installation of get_iplayer.
