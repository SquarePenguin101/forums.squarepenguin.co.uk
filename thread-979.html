If you have the PIDs or URLs for specific programmes that generate "Segment not found" warnings, post them in this thread. 06-08-2016, 12:12 PM
Thanks everyone for your submissions. They have been very helpful in assessing this problem. I'm closing this thread for now, before Olympics programmes begin to come online.

It seems clear that the BBC is producing corrupt HLS media streams across the board. There is no way to know if the missing segments are due to production errors or server problems at the CDNs,  but it doesn't really matter from the perspective of a get_iplayer user. For now, use the hvf recording modes. If this continues much longer, a new release of get_iplayer, with hvf as the default recording modes, will be necessary.
