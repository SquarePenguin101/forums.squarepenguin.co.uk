For a series pid
https://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/m0005k5...des/player
To download a series add --pid-recursive

get_iplayer --type=radio --pid-recursive --pid=m0005k5m

for an individual episode
https://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/m0005svw
get_iplayer --type=radio --pid=m0005svw
