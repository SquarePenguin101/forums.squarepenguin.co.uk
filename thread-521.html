Hi Derek,

As Dinky indicated a solution (one that worked for me) is to use the --modes=hlsbest switch on your command line (or in your batch file).  It has been working great for almost 24 hours now on my home server.  

Here's one line from the batch file that I wrote to automatically update my content:
perl.exe get_iplayer.pl --pvr-add=BBC_One_MCGaS "More Creatures Great And Small" --modes=hlsbest --type=tv --channel="BBC One" --file-prefix="<nameshort><-senum><-episodeshort>"

I hope this helps.
