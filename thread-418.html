Is there a way of exporting and importing these?

user-2

03-06-2015, 01:27 PM
No. However, all your personal settings (and your programme data cache files) are contained in a single profile directory, so you can copy the contents of that directory (or restore from backup) to the same location on another machine. The default location is
Code:
$HOME/.get_iplayer
(Linux/Unix/OSX) or
Code:
%USERPROFILE%\.get_iplayer
(Windows). You can use a non-default location with
Code:
--profile-dir
, but I don't recommend it.
