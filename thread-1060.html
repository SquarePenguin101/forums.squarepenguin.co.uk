Seems like there's a categorisation error by the beeb as it's telling you that the only version available is a signed version i.e. there's someone standing to the side of the screen literally signing the spoken content for those who are hard of hearing/deaf.

Having just downloaded this version to test there's clearly no signing going on so if you want this programme then just add --versions=signed to your command and it'll download the signing free version for you.
