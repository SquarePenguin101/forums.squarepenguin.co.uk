There is no way to know. It's nothing to do with get_iplayer. Some users have reported much faster streaming over high speed connections, so it's something particular to your situation. It may not be coincidental that 2500 kbps is roughly the bandwidth required for HD streaming from the iPlayer site. If you never ever get better than that, then perhaps your broadband provider is interpreting "up to 50 Mb" rather loosely.
