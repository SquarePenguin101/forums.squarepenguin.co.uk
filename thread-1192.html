From a cmd line window you could just start the Web-PVR option
open up your browser to http://localhost:1935 and run a search for "King of Mars"...
Then select "Add Serch to PVR":

Code:
fields name
subtitles 0
thumb 0
type radio
search0 King of Mars

That will create and PVR entry do download everything from the radio with the words "King of Mars" which are the 6 episodes you are looking for.

Then just run

get-iplayer --pvr

and it will download them.
