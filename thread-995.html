As I understand your question and the situation:

You should leave the laptop running until the series download completes. Switching off mid-stream will halt the download.

A recent thread suggests there are problems with resuming incomplete downloads. I think you would have to be very lucky to switch off after downloading and post-processing one programme and before the next programme in the series starts to download.

If, however, you use sleep, or hibernate, the download should continue when you resurrect your session.
