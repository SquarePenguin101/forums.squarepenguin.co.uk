How do I find the series PID for Informer?

I thought this would download all six episodes but it only downloaded one episode,

get_iplayer --tvmode best --pid-recursive --pid p06lhrf9 --output d:\temp\tv

Do i find the series pid from the url on the iPlayer website

user-1496

06-11-2018, 12:48 AM
Yep, you got the series PID wrong, you used the PID for episode 6.

If you are at the iPlayer site and on the episode page, click the link for "Programme website". The URL of that page is https://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/p06lh6tf and p06lh6tf is the series PID. You will find the whole series will now download if you use that PID instead of the one you used (with the exception of episode 6 as you have already downloaded that one).
