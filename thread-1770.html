Thanks for posting the logs, but you really should read them first. If you had, you would have seen a lot of this:

get_iplayer Output:

ERROR: Response: 500 Can't connect to www.bbc.co.uk:80

If your system either can't or won't allow get_iplayer to connect to BBC sites, there is nothing it can do. Only you will know what has been done to your system. It's always a good idea to start by blaming your antivirus. The problem could also be upstream. Again, nothing get_iplayer can do about it.
