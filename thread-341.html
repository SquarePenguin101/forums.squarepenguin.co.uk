Moved to mobile, but get_iplayer ignores Flash mobile streams.
Code:
flashstd
is 640x360 but almost half the bitrate of
Code:
flashhigh
and roughly the same size as
Code:
flashlow
. If you really want the smaller video size, use
Code:
hlslow
for now.
