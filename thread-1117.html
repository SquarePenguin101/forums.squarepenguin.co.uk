get_iplayer --history returns no results for downloaded programmes

user-1489

07-12-2016, 02:03 PM
At some point in recent months the WebPVRs "Recordings" tab seems to have stopped working - empty content and searches for programs by name or PID or any other method no longer works, despite their being quite a lot of historical downloaded content and the PVR itself continuing to download new content. I'm afraid that I did not notice when it happened.

The PVR tab's list of programs I'm downloading is present and appears to be accurate.
The PVR's Search tab is present and shows current BBC content as expected.


The "list" of downloaded programs is physically present at C:\Users\user_name\.get_iplayer\pvr and the Options file correctly specifies the download directory etc. so I'm very puzzled at the absence of programs within the WebPVR.

Is there an "approved" way to rebuild the Recordings list and force the WebPVR to display them?

What do you mean by "empty content"? Do recent downloads continue to appear in the Recordings tab? Do you know the programmes you're looking for actually exist in the download history? Are you certain you haven't set any options that break your searches?
Quote:The "list" of downloaded programs is physically present at C:\Users\user_name\.get_iplayer\pvr
That is not the list of downloaded programmes. That is the directory containing pvr searches. You want C:\Users\user_name\.get_iplayer\download_history. It's worth taking a look - the file may have been corrupted somehow. If so, removing corrupted entries may help.
Quote:Is there an "approved" way to rebuild the Recordings list and force the WebPVR to display them?
No, but see this thread: http://www.mail-archive.com/get_iplayer@...09474.html
