Failed to download programme schedule for BBC Three

user-2

04-04-2016, 04:52 PM
The BBC has removed the schedule data for BBC Three, which is now online only. Consequently, you may safely ignore all appearances of the warning message below during a cache refresh:
Code:
WARNING: Failed to download programme schedule http://www.bbc.co.uk/bbcthree/programmes/schedules/this_week.xml
To prevent the warning from appearing at all, you can use --refresh-exclude="BBC Three". BBC Three will be removed from cache refreshes in the next release.
