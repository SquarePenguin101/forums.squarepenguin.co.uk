03-05-2017, 07:49 PM
Take your download history from GiA:

Code:
/Users/<username>/Library/Application Support/Get iPlayer Automator/download_history

If you haven't yet done any get_iplayer downloads, copy it to:

Code:
/Users/<username>/.get_iplayer/download_history

If you have already done some get_iplayer downloads, use a text editor to insert the contents of your GiA download history at the top of the get_iplayer download history file. Don't leave blank lines between old and new data.
