The other episodes of Afternoon Drama are too old to be indexed by get_iplayer.  Read:

https://github.com/get-iplayer/get_iplay...i/faq#faq1

This will be improved a bit in the next release.

The name of the pvr job doesn't matter, though it should reflect the contents. You would also need to change the value of the search0 field to the desired value.
