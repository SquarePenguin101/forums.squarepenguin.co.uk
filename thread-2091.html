This has nothing to do with PIDs. Using --get just turns your search criteria into PIDs. Errors like this for one stream out of 36 available, for one programme out of tens of thousands, mean nothing. This is the internet - downloads sometimes fail, upstream resources may not always be available. That's why get_iplayer tries all available streams, subject to your settings. If the stream shown is the only stream attempted, then your settings are very messed up. If that is the case, follow our instructions to provide a proper report so we can see what is happening on your system.
