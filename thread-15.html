I have moved this to a new topic since it has nothing to with the issue covered in the topic where it was originally posted.

The installer is perfectly compatible with Windows 7. If the installer fails the integrity check, it means it has been corrupted somehow. There is no way for us to know what is causing the problem on your machine. It could be your browser, your antivirus, your download manager, etc. The file is not corrupted on the server, so something went wrong when you downloaded it. Try downloading again, or perhaps download on a different machine if one is available. Google the error message (in English) and you'll find a number of results, though I can't say that any of them will provide a solution. The installer is built with NSIS, and this is what the NSIS site has to say on the issue:

http://nsis.sourceforge.net/Why_do_I_get_NSIS_Error
