Don't put reams of repetitive, unreadable text in your post. Use the attachment facility.

Your connections to the BBC servers are being blocked. The most likely culprit for your problem is your anti-virus package, if you use one. If so, disable it completely and try again. Another possible suspect would be your firewall, but it's not terribly likely that you would have blocked outbound connections. If you've been messing around with your firewall, it's worth checking. The problem may also be upstream from your computer, but it's usually an anti-virus package at fault.

As with the original thread, something is denying rtmpdump network access. It could be anti-spyware, anti-virus not completely disabled, upstream problems, duff VPN - anything. What it's not is anything to do with get_iplayer or rtmpdump. Now that you've moved to a completely different system, it's no surprise results will be different. The logical question for you is: what's different between the systems that would affect network access? We can't possibly know how you've configured your systems.
