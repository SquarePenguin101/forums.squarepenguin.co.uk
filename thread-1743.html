(12-04-2018, 07:41 PM)As a test I just forced Galapagos and that one is still 1280x720.

Are you aware you can use the --info switch to see what modes are available, it's got to be faster than forcing a download.

Launch get_iplayer
From the command line find the index number for that episode of Nature's Microworlds
type: get_iplayer microworlds
For me the Galapagos episode has the index number 1619
type: get_iplayer --info 1619
The output will contain the available modes and their (estiamated) sizes.
