I'm not sure what you're talking about here. Pasting URLs in the Search field would never work unless "Search in" is set to "Web Page" - which I'm guess you don't do - and there is no "Download" to click. I'll assume you mean that you pasted the URL in the Quick URL field then clicked Record. In that case, the BBC broke it. Until get_iplayer is patched, paste the URL in the Search field (be sure to trim trailing spaces), set "Search in" to "Web Page", check BBC Radio in Programme type, click Search, then click the Record link for your programme in the search results. You can also put just the PID (p027b349) in the Search and set "Search in" to "Pid" to get the same result. And for the time being, be sure to do it within 7 days of broadcast. It's not yet clear whether programmes will still be searchable after 7 days even they are now available for longer on the iPlayer site.
