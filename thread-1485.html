23-08-2017, 09:46 AM
You can just use the PID method to download the programme directly.

If you haven't been continually refreshing the cache once a week then get_iplayer won't be able to build up a 30 day cache through which to search for programmes - it can only obtain the last 7 days during any single cache refresh so must be triggered once a week to 'remember' further back than that.
