Sorry, you're on your own with VPNs and proxies. The "No specified modes" error is a fairly reliable indicator of geo-blocking, but all I can offer is the obvious: all VPNs and proxies are not equal. Some may be blocked and some may not provide a working IP even though in the UK. And even if you have managed to slip through the net and access the programme on the iPlayer site, that doesn't necessarily mean get_iplayer can access the programme.
