The output is telling you what is wrong, as you alluded to:

INFO: No versions of this programme were selected (available versions: shortened)

It's telling you the available version is of type 'shortened', and you haven't selected to download a version of that type.

The Recording Options section of the Options page in the wiki explains how to select different versions (it's the last entry in that section of the table).

You can't extrapolate from a single data point. This morning's "From Our Own Correspondent" is just the opposite: the podcast version is currently missing. Reserve judgement at least until the shortened version of "In Our Time" has been broadcast tonight. They may simply be tardy in production.  
(08-11-2018, 01:53 PM)Up'til now I have shunned podcasts because they have been in mono for the lower bit rate versions that I like to download.
Don't confuse the audio format in the "podcast" version downloaded by get_iplayer and the audio in the MP3 file offered in the podcast feed. They are not the same.
(08-11-2018, 01:53 PM)Now I'll have to configure my options to accept them:
Code:
radiomode worse
versions original,shortened,podcast
For everyone else: Don't set versionlist in preferences as above. It is redundant and a potential source of errors, especially if you also download TV programmes. There may be a few cases where you prefer the podcast version of a particular radio programme, but use presets or ad hoc options for those. By default, you don't need any special configuration if the podcast is the only version available, as in this isolated case. If it should happen that you get an errant podcast version you don't want, just force a re-download if/when the original version becomes available.
