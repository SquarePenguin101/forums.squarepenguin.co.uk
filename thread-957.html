If you search for "Have I got news" like so:

get_iplayer "Have I got news"

...you'll get a result like this:

get_iplayer Output:

Matches:
930:    Have I Got News for You: Series 50 - Episode 2, BBC One, b06h8xzq

INFO: 1 Matching Programmes

You can see in the title that the show name is appended with "Series 50". 

So if you use a search term like "Have I Got News for You: Series 50" in your PVR entry you'll only get episodes from that series.
